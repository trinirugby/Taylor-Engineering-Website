Why Taylor Engineering

What Sets Us Apart

40+ Years of Proven Experience

Founded in 1984 by E. Liston Taylor, a Chief Engineer with two decades of prior dockyard experience. Taylor Engineering has operated continuously through every era of Caribbean maritime commerce — from traditional cargo trades to modern FPSO and offshore oil operations.

Broadest Caribbean Reach

Active survey coverage from St. Maarten in the north to Suriname and Guyana in the south — supported by partnerships and ongoing engagement in Barbados, Guyana, and Suriname. A single-source solution for multi-port Caribbean operations.

Specialist P&I Expertise

Over 15 years of specialist expertise in the safe transportation of Direct Reduced Iron (DRI A/B/C fines), Hot Briquetted Iron (HBI), and iron-ore fines to the United States and globally. Trusted by P&I clubs, underwriters, and oil majors for complex claims and dispute resolution.

100% Local Ownership & Staffing

Taylor Engineering Agencies Limited is 100% owned by nationals of Trinidad and Tobago, and our staff are all nationals of Trinidad and Tobago. Deep regional knowledge means faster mobilisation and more accurate reports.
