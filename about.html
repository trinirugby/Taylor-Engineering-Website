About Taylor Engineering

Our Story

Taylor Engineering Agencies Limited is a family-owned firm of marine surveyors operating since 1984. The firm was founded by E. Liston Taylor, a Chief Engineer with two decades of dockyard experience, and has built a reputation as a leading provider of marine surveying and consultancy services in the Caribbean.

From inception, the firm has primarily worked on behalf of shipowners, charterers, underwriters, and international companies — consistently upholding the highest professional standards and unwavering integrity. Today, Taylor Engineering is the most diversified marine surveying and consultancy company in the Caribbean and represents numerous international organisations, including P&I clubs, Hull & Machinery underwriters, oil majors, IOPC Funds, shipowners, and charterers.
