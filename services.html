What We Do

A Full-Spectrum Marine Surveying and Consultancy Firm

From vessel compliance audits to complex cargo claims — Taylor Engineering provides the Caribbean’s broadest range of specialist marine surveying disciplines.

01

Marine Assurance & Compliance

  • Vessel assurance audits (Flag, Class, SOLAS, MARPOL, ISM, ISPS; safety-critical systems, navigation, propulsion, DP, crew certifications)
  • OVID inspections (OCIMF) & CMID inspections (IMCA)
  • ISM compliance surveys with corrective-action support
  • DP assurance surveys (functionality, operator logs, redundancy)
  • Navigation equipment surveys (SOLAS-compliant)
  • Hull and structural surveys
  • Voyage performance surveys (fuel, speed, passage execution)
  • Accident & incident investigations with root-cause analysis
02

Cargo Operations & Loadout Oversight

  • Fuel (MGO/IFO) loadouts & ROB surveys — three-stage sampling, bacterial testing, Bunker Delivery Note verification
  • Liquid bulk (base oils, chemicals, mud) — pre/post gauging, manifold sampling, leak checks
  • Brine loadouts (CaCl₂, NaBr) — dedicated lines, salinity & density monitoring, strict in-house protocol
  • Specialty liquids (methanol, DMO) — FPSO-use cargo, compatibility & sample retention
  • Dry bulk (cement, barite) — calibrated silo/weighbridge measurement, contamination prevention
  • Cargo handling surveys — gear, stowage, vessel stability
  • Container, break-bulk & specialised cargoes, including reefer integrity and petrochemical/off-spec investigations
  • DRI/HBI/Fines specialist loading (15+ years, to the United States and globally)
  • Draft surveys & quantity/quality dispute resolution
03

Hull & Machinery Surveys

  • Hull plating, decks, and internal structures
  • Coating failure, corrosion, and structural damage assessment
  • Ultrasonic hatch cover weathertight testing
  • Watertight integrity testing of vessel ramps
  • UT-thickness measurement consultancy
  • Photographic reports with repair recommendations
04

P&I & Cargo Surveys

  • Cargo damage investigations
  • Draft surveys (bill-of-lading verification)
  • On-hire / off-hire surveys with bunker ROB certification
  • Quantity & quality dispute resolution through sampling, testing, and analysis
  • Petrochemical & off-spec cargo investigations
  • Container, break-bulk & specialised cargo surveys (incl. reefer integrity)
05

Tank Cleaning & Inspection

  • Tank cleaning oversight using a timeline matrix — waste quantification (slops, residues, rags)
  • Solid & liquid waste removal — ship-side and shore-side quantification
  • Post-cleaning tank inspections with photographic certification
  • Borescope inspections of cargo lines, manifolds, and crossovers — company-owned equipment, deployable on short notice without third-party hire
  • Water sampling & contaminant analysis
  • Available stand-alone or bundled with On-Hire surveys
06

Marine Consultancy & Claims

  • Accident & incident investigation with root-cause analysis
  • Claims handling & settlement — cargo damage, delay, pollution, vessel damage, personal injury
  • Crew & vessel welfare — medical emergencies, complex repatriation following treatment, repatriation of mortal remains, stowaway repatriation
  • Environmental & MARPOL compliance (oily water separators, garbage management, spill response)
  • Ballast Water Management surveys
  • Marine consultancy with Master Mariner senior surveyor for assurance and DP trials
